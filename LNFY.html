Lost n Found Youth

Lost-n-Found Youth (LNFY) is a nonprofit organization that works to end homelessness for LGBTQ youth by providing food, shelter and life stabilization services. We were inspired to choose LNFY because it is a local organization working to uplift an at-risk and underserved community. Providing local youths with support systems and essential services to sustain and improve their lives is ultimately what pushed us to choose this organization.
